Yummy Pockets: Delicious Dumplings (Jiaozi)

Dumplings are tasty little pockets filled with meat and veggies! They're a special food eaten during celebrations like Spring Festival.

Racing Dragons: The Dragon Boat Festival

Watch out for the dragon boats! This festival features exciting races in long boats decorated like colorful dragons.

Elegant Strokes: Chinese Ink Painting

Using black ink and brushes, artists create beautiful paintings of nature, like mountains, flowers, and animals. It's a very special art form.

Picture Words: Interesting Chinese Characters

Instead of letters, Chinese uses characters! Each one can mean a whole word or idea. See if you can spot the character for "person" (人)!

The Big Celebration: Spring Festival (Chinese New Year)

Spring Festival is like a super big New Year celebration in China! People enjoy special foods, colorful decorations, and exciting dragon dances.

Amazing Moves: The Art of Kung Fu

Kung Fu is a Chinese martial art with incredible moves that also teaches respect and focus. You might have seen it in movies!
