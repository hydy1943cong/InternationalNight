Reaching the Top: The Himalayas and Mount Everest

The Himalayas are the world's tallest mountains, and Mount Everest is the very top – the highest point on Earth, always covered in snow!

Unique Pillars: The Zhangjiajie Mountains

Imagine tall, thin rock pillars covered in green, like something from the movie Avatar! These mountains are truly special.

A Giant River: The Long Yangtze

The Yangtze River is one of the longest rivers in the world, flowing like a huge blue ribbon across China, helping with travel and farming.

The Yellow River: A River of Color

The Yellow River gets its color from the yellow soil it carries. It's very important for farming and has a long history in China.

Ancient Grandeur: Beijing

Beijing is China's capital city with amazing old palaces like the Forbidden City, where emperors used to live a long, long time ago.

Skyscrapers and Lights: Shanghai

Shanghai is a super modern city with tall, shiny buildings called skyscrapers that reach up to the sky! It's a busy and exciting place.
